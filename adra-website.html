About Adra

Redefining how students learn—not by rote memorization, but by understanding concepts from first principles.

❌ The Problem: Rote Learning

Traditional education focuses on **memorization** without a deep understanding of the underlying concepts. This leads to poor retention, inability to apply knowledge in new contexts, and a lack of critical thinking skills.

✅ The Solution: Conceptual Mastery

Adra combines **AI-driven conversational learning** with the **Feynman Technique**. By encouraging students to explain concepts back, Adra ensures true, lasting conceptual understanding and mastery.

How It Works

A simple, four-step process to achieve true conceptual mastery with your AI mentor.

1

📚 Integrate Content

Students upload their class or subject PDFs. Adra instantly processes the content, preparing for an interactive session.

2

🤖 Engage in Dialogue

Adra explains each page conversationally, asking "why" and "how" questions to build understanding from first principles.

3

🗣️ Feynman Technique

The AI asks the student to explain the concept back in simple terms, identifying and filling any gaps in their knowledge.

4

✨ Adaptive Mastery

Adra adapts the pace and depth of the lesson in real-time, ensuring personalized feedback and guaranteed conceptual clarity.
